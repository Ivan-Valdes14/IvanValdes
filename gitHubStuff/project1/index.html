About Me

Hey there! I'm Ivan Valdes, a current senior at UNF studying Information Science, with a side of Marketing. I started off thinking about a business degree, but found it didn't grab me enough on its own. That's when I realized I had a knack for computing and problem-solving, which made Information Science seem like the perfect blend of both my interests.

I'm a South Florida guy, originally from a town called Southwest Ranches. It was a pretty peaceful place to grow up, not a lot of neighbors, but it meant I learned how to keep myself entertained. This involved a lot of outdoor adventures like "hunting" lizards or just being out in nature. I played a ton of sports in high school - Ice Hockey, American Football - you name it. If it involved a bit of rough and tumble, I was there. I also got pretty far with martial arts, almost made it to black belt before college got in the way. And if you look closely, you might spot the gunshot scar on my arm from an accident in high school - pretty wild, right?
